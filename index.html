August, 2020 The airline industry was severely impacted by the COVID-19 pandemic. Being able to track consumer sentiment towards the airline industry can help airlines understand when we may be turning the corner and ready for a bounceback.

April, 2020 As customer needs grow more and more complex, businesses require an ever deeper understanding of their customers in order to boost customer retention and brand loyalty. Anonymized product reviews provide a treasure grove of data for companies to better understand their customers.

Predicting Airbnb Prices

December, 2019 While we have seen a meteoric rise in the number of Airbnb stays, hosts of Airbnb accommodations face the dilemma of setting optimal prices for traveler stays. This project explores different machine learning models to understand how best to predict Airbnb prices.
